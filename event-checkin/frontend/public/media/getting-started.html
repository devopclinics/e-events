Step 2 of 7

Create your event

In Event Setup, click New Event. Enter your event name, host, date & time, and base URL. Hit Save. You'll see sections for Start here, Guests, Invites & RSVP, Seating, Orders, Entry areas, Deliveries, Gift list, and Team — everything you need in one place.

💡 Turn on features like Seating, Orders, Entry areas, Deliveries & Gift list from Team & settings → Features.

Step 3 of 7

Add your guest list

Go to Start here → Download template — it has exactly the columns your event uses. Fill it in and Upload guest file. Or paste a Google Sheets link and set a sync interval. Re-importing never duplicates guests — it fills in missing details instead.

💡 Add a tags column (e.g. "VIP; Press") to automatically set entry access for each guest.

Step 4 of 7

Send invitations on
every channel

In Invites & RSVP, set up your RSVP page — pick anyone with the event link or personal invite links. Then send via WhatsApp, SMS, or Email. Guests get a branded invite with their personal QR ticket on confirmation — no app needed.

📱 WhatsApp is free for guests. SMS/WhatsApp sending uses message credits — email is always free.

Step 5 of 7

Track RSVPs &
approvals in real time

The Guests tab shows every person: Attending, Declined, Pending, or No Reply — plus their check-in status on the day. If you turned on Approval required, approve or reject RSVPs one by one, or Approve all. Use Broadcast to push updates (reminders, schedule changes) to any group.

📣 Broadcast targets: All guests · Attending · Declined · No-reply · Checked-in · Not checked-in.

Step 6 of 7

Check-in day —
just scan & go

Set the event to Active in Start here, then open Check-in on any phone. Point the camera at each guest's QR — admission is instant. No app install needed. For Entry areas events, staff pick their gate once and every scan automatically checks the guest into the right area.

🔐 VIP zones, backstage, restricted areas — each gate enforces ticket type & tag rules automatically.

in Results

📊 Post-event: venue analytics show peak arrival times, room-to-room flow, and each guest's journey.
