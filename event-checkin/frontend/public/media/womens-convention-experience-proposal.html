The organizer problem

Manual lists and paper checks make it hard to know who arrived, who signed consent, who collected gifts, which room they belong in, and which sessions they attended.

The Festio answer

Every guest receives a personal pass. Staff scan it and see the next required steps, while organizers monitor completion across check-in, consent, seating, gifts, and sessions.

Positioning: Festio is not replacing the convention program. It is the operations system that keeps the program moving.
